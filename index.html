Conjecture Prover

Submit a mathematical conjecture. Get a Lean 4 verified proof.

We run an automated pipeline that takes your conjecture in plain mathematical language and attempts to produce a formal proof in Lean 4 with Mathlib. Multiple AI models collaborate to generate natural-language proofs, formalize them, and verify correctness through the Lean proof assistant.

What you get back

If proved A Lean 4 proof file, LaTeX paper, and PDF — machine-verified.
If disproved A formal counterexample or negation proof with explanation.
If open The formal statement in Lean 4 plus numerical experiments supporting or refuting your conjecture.
Always A response to every submission — you will hear back regardless of outcome.

How it works

Your conjecture → NL proof sketch → Multi-model debate → Lean 4 formalization → Verification & repair (up to 5 attempts) → Semantic alignment check → Negation guard → Results emailed to you

Example input

"For every integer n, n&sup5; − n is divisible by 30."

You can use plain text, LaTeX notation ($\beta$, \mathbb{R}), or a mix. The more precise your statement, the better the result.
